West Cork Rally 2016

Get your ticket now!

Event Info

The West Cork Rally is a two day international car rally, run over 14 stages of rugged coastline and countryside.

The event boasts one of the largest entries of a two day rally in the British Isles and has a large percentage of overseas entries, peaking with 70 out of 200 entries coming from abroad.

Based in the picturesque tourist town of Clonakilty since its inception 37 years ago, the West Cork Rally signals the kick-off of the tourist season in the area. Visitors to Clonakilty can enjoy numerous award winning restaurants, pubs and hotels, along with the natural beauty of its beaches and countryside.

Key Details

Date: 12 - 13 March 2016
