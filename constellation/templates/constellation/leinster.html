Event Info

Digital Biscuit is always an interesting proposition. Looking at the point at which technology and culture collide, it’s of huge value to anybody with a penchant for film, television, or tech.

Previous years have seen directors Michel Gondry and David Yates speak at the event, as well as creators like David Chase, and producers such as Borgen’s Camilla Hammerich featuring.

If you are keen on film and television and the business behind it, you will really love Digital Biscuit. It’s a brilliant occasion that brings professionals together and looks at the future on the silver and small screens.

Key Details

Date: 20 - 22 October 2016
Location: Point Village, Dublin
